# Food-Adulteration-
Your Food is Lying to You.
A live, no-BS consumer alert system for Nepal's food adulteration crisis.
→ https://debesh-triyog.github.io/Food-Adulteration-/

Nepal's food supply is chemically compromised. Official DFTQC tests found caustic soda, hydrogen peroxide, and sulfur compounds in milk sold to your family. Meanwhile, multinationals use green packaging, fake "CREME" spelling, and child-targeted ads to sell you palm oil and sugar water disguised as healthy food. This website cuts through the lies: it teaches you to spot fake ORS (deadly sugar water), decode "Flavor Only" labels (zero real ingredients), and buy actual dairy instead of vegetable fat in a fancy packet.

No paywall. No ads. No academic fluff. Just a 30-second checklist you can use at any Kirana store in Nepal. Every claim comes from government data, every trick is documented with real examples, and every protection tip works on the ground in Kalimati, Ason, or your local bazaar.

Share it with every parent you know. If you can code, fork it and improve it. If you can't, just use it. The food industry is counting on your confusion. This is the antidote.
